Ritchie Valens

A singer who should be known.

Richard Valenzuela...

  • May 1941 - Is born in Los Angeles, California
  • October 1957 - Joined a band called "The Silhouettes"
  • May 1958 - Signed a record contract with Del-Fi Records. He changed his name to Ritchie Valens
  • July 1958 - Released his first single "Come On Let's Go"
  • October 1958 - Appeared on American Bandstand, a popular tv show
  • October 1958 - Released mega-hits "La Bamba" and "Donna"
  • December 1958 - Appeared on American Bandstand again
  • January 1959 - Went on tour with many famous musicians
  • February 1959 - Died in horrible plane crash
  • May 1990 - Is honored with a Star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame
  • March 2001 - Is inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame
